# Warthogs
Warthogs is an add-on that adds these fabulous animals to the game, increasing the available wildlife as well as food sources, and they can even be integrated into your farms.

These animals spawn in plains and savannas. They usually have many offspring, and if you get too close to them, they can become aggressive. However, if you approach them with an apple or a beetroot in your hand, the warthog will calm down and you'll be able to take it to your farm, and even breed them. They drop their own type of meat, which can obviously be cooked.

This add-on is compatible with and follows the mechanics of my [Realistic Livestock](https://github.com/elosoarcade/realistic-lifestock) add-on, so warthogs grow 30 times slower than other animals and generate manure that can be turned into bonemeal and coal.

❤️ Health: 20 | ⚔️ Attack: 3 | ✨ EXP: 1-3

This addon is optimized to run effectively on Realms and dedicated servers with minimal hardware resource consumption and its available in english and español. 🇬🇧 🇲🇽
